E-Commerce SEO Case Study Overview

Box & Bloom is a boutique gift box e-commerce site built on WordPress and WooCommerce. This case study focuses on the storefront build and the e-commerce SEO work behind category content, metadata direction, internal linking, trust pages, and launch-ready on-page improvements.

E-Commerce SEO Objectives

complete the storefront structure
prepare metadata coverage
strengthen category-page content
improve trust and internal linking
finish launch-readiness SEO checks

E-Commerce SEO and On-Page Work Completed

  • Built and refined the WordPress/WooCommerce storefront structure.
  • Reviewed and improved page layout, hierarchy, and internal linking.
  • Prepared homepage and shop metadata direction for stronger e-commerce SEO coverage.
  • Added and refined category descriptions for key store sections to improve topical coverage and on-page support.
  • Strengthened footer trust-page linking and page completeness.
  • Audited product content quality including titles, descriptions, and alt text.
  • Finalized launch-readiness checks for SEO plugin setup, canonicals, sitemap, noindex, and permalinks.

Key E-Commerce SEO Improvements

  • Metadata readiness was improved for the homepage, shop, products, and category templates.
  • Canonical and sitemap behavior was prepared through SEO-plugin configuration planning and validation.
  • Search-result indexation handling and launch controls were reviewed before publication.
  • Trust pages and footer links were strengthened to support both usability and store credibility.
  • Category support content reduced thin-archive risk and improved topical coverage across key store sections.

E-Commerce SEO Case Study Outcome

The site reached launch-ready status with a stronger technical SEO foundation, cleaner trust-page coverage, improved category content, and finalized admin-side SEO controls. The result was a more credible and indexable WooCommerce store prepared for submission to search engines and post-launch monitoring.
