Project Overview

Box & Bloom is a boutique gift box e-commerce site built on WordPress and WooCommerce. The project involved both storefront implementation and SEO optimization, with the goal of turning the site into a polished, launch-ready store with stronger crawlability, clearer content architecture, improved trust signals, and better on-page SEO coverage.

Initial Challenges

  • Theme structure was mostly sound, but launch-critical SEO controls still depended on admin configuration.
  • Trust pages needed to be live and linked properly.
  • Category pages needed supporting descriptions to avoid thin archive content.
  • Product quality varied at the content level, including titles, descriptions, and image alt text.
  • Search-result indexation and plugin-managed metadata still needed final configuration.

Work Completed

  • Built and refined the WordPress/WooCommerce storefront structure.
  • Reviewed and improved page layout, hierarchy, and internal linking.
  • Prepared homepage and shop metadata direction.
  • Added and refined category descriptions for key store sections.
  • Strengthened footer trust-page linking and page completeness.
  • Audited product content quality including titles, descriptions, and alt text.
  • Finalized launch-readiness checks for SEO plugin setup, canonicals, sitemap, noindex, and permalinks.

Technical SEO Focus

Metadata readiness for homepage, shop, products, and categories
Canonical and XML sitemap management through SEO plugin configuration
Noindex handling for internal search-result pages
Improved internal linking and page trust signals
Category-page quality improvements to reduce thin-content risk
Final launch QA for crawlability, indexation settings, and live-page integrity

Outcome

The site reached launch-ready status with a stronger technical SEO foundation, cleaner trust-page coverage, improved category support content, and finalized admin-side SEO controls. The result was a more credible and indexable WooCommerce store prepared for submission to search engines and post-launch monitoring.
