Technical SEO Audit Overview

This technical SEO audit combines crawl data, metadata analysis, response-code checks, and performance validation. The goal was to identify strengths, recurring issues, and practical areas for further technical SEO cleanup without overstating the findings.

Technical SEO Audit Objectives

review crawl coverage
assess page-title quality
measure meta-description coverage
validate response codes
check performance and Core Web Vitals

Audit Work Completed

  • Ran a crawl to collect URL coverage, indexability, and metadata signals across the site.
  • Reviewed title tags for duplication, length issues, and formatting consistency.
  • Analyzed meta-description coverage and identified missing-description patterns.
  • Validated response-code behavior to confirm accessibility and the absence of broken internal pages in the crawl sample.
  • Reviewed PageSpeed Insights and Core Web Vitals outputs to understand performance quality and optimization opportunities.

Key Technical SEO Findings

  • The crawl showed strong accessibility overall, with crawled pages returning valid responses and remaining indexable.
  • Title tags showed duplication and length issues that could reduce search-result clarity.
  • A meaningful portion of pages lacked meta descriptions, which weakened snippet control.
  • Response-code analysis did not reveal notable 4xx or 5xx problems in the crawl sample.
  • Performance and Core Web Vitals results were strong, giving the site a stable technical base from a user-experience perspective.

Technical SEO Audit Outcome

The audit confirmed that the website had a stable crawl and performance foundation, while also surfacing on-page cleanup opportunities around titles and meta descriptions. The result was a balanced technical SEO assessment covering both discoverability and search-result presentation quality.
